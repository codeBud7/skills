# 1on1-log — Radical Candor and post-meeting headings

Use with [`SKILL.md`](SKILL.md) for retrieval, saving, and gates.

## Radical Candor

Reuse the stance from [`1on1/reference.md`](../1on1/reference.md).

## Post-meeting block structure

Append under `---` and `# Post-meeting log` in the same brief file. Use short paragraphs under these headings:

`Summary`

`Key discussion threads`

`Decisions made`

`Your commitments`

`Direct's action items`

`Signals`

`Loop closure`

`Carry-forward follow-ups`

`Sources`

### Header metadata (required)

Before `Summary`, include:

- Generated at (timestamp)
- Meeting title and time
- Granola meeting ID
- Notion page URL (only when written)

### Section rules

Under **`Summary`**: 2–4 tight lines on what the 1:1 was really about — not a transcript recap.

Under **`Key discussion threads`**: substance of the back-and-forth — what was debated, clarified, or pushed on. Tag with `[transcript]` or `[Granola summary]` when not from full transcript.

Under **`Decisions made`**: explicit decisions only — omit if none.

Under **`Your commitments`**: manager action items, separate from the direct's. Use checklist bullets.

Under **`Direct's action items`**: the direct's commitments only. Use checklist bullets.

Under **`Signals`**: morale, capacity, energy — **grounded in what was explicitly said or observed** in the transcript. No diagnosis, no verdicts; evaluative reads phrased as questions. Sensitive personal disclosures (health, family, leave plans) go under a labeled **`Personal / sensitive`** subsection within `Signals` so they are easy to spot or redact.

Under **`Loop closure`**: compare against the same-day prep brief and feedback note when present — which planned topics were covered, which feedback was delivered, what slipped. One-line cross-link to the feedback note file when it exists.

Under **`Carry-forward follow-ups`**: unresolved items for the **next** 1:1 brief. This section is the primary handoff to the next `1on1` run.

Under **`Sources`**: Granola transcript (or summary fallback), brief file, feedback note file, Notion (if written).

Use lightweight source tags like `[transcript]`, `[brief]`, `[feedback-note]`, `[Granola summary]`.

## Style

Short paragraphs. No tables. Facts over interpretation.

Match the direct's feedback preference from their profile when known.
